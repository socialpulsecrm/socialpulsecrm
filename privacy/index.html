Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you connect a social media account (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Threads, Mastodon), we collect and store OAuth tokens (encrypted at rest) and basic profile information (username, display name, follower count) necessary to publish content on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide our service: scheduling posts, publishing content to your connected accounts, and displaying analytics from your own accounts. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

All OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM. Data is stored in secured PostgreSQL databases. We follow industry-standard security practices.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with social media platform APIs (Meta, Google, X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, TikTok, Mastodon) solely to perform actions you explicitly request (posting, reading your analytics).

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You can disconnect any account at any time, which removes stored tokens. You may request full account deletion by contacting us.

6. Contact
