Facebook Data Deletion

Last updated: July 12, 2026

How to Disconnect App Account Data Deletion Questions

Social Pulse integrates with Facebook using Meta's secure API services to help you schedule posts, manage messages, and track analytics. If you want to revoke our application's access permissions to your Facebook account or request that your cached data be deleted, follow the instructions below.

1. How to Disconnect Social Pulse from Facebook

You can revoke Social Pulse's access to your Facebook profile or pages at any time directly through your Facebook account settings. Doing so immediately disables our app's ability to fetch or interact with your Facebook data:

  • Go to your Facebook profile's Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  • In the left sidebar menu, click on Apps and Websites.
  • Find Social Pulse in the list of active integrations and click on it.
  • Click the Remove button.
  • Optional: In the pop-up confirmation dialog, choose whether to delete all posts, videos, or events that Social Pulse has posted on your timeline on your behalf.
  • Click Remove again to confirm the action.

2. How to Request Deletion of Your Account Data

Disconnecting Social Pulse from Facebook revokes active permissions. If you also want to permanently delete your Social Pulse user account, connected social tokens, and cached dashboard data from our secure databases, you have two options:

  • Self-Service In-App Deletion: Log in to your account at app.socialpulsecrm.com. Navigate to Settings > Account Settings, and scroll to the bottom. Click Delete Account. This will immediately queue your account and all associated OAuth tokens for permanent removal.
  • Manual Support Request: If you are unable to log in, send an email to our support team at support@socialpulsecrm.com or privacy@socialpulsecrm.com with the subject line Facebook Data Deletion Request. Please include the registered email address of your Social Pulse account. We will verify and process your deletion request within 30 days.

3. Questions & Contact

If you have any questions about this process, our data handling policies, or how your social media account integrations are managed, please review our full Privacy Policy or reach out directly to our privacy team at privacy@socialpulsecrm.com.
